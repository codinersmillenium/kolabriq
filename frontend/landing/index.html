Our process

Kolabriq simplifies project management from start to finish, backed by AI and blockchain. Each step supports secure, productive, and fair collaboration.

1. Project Starts with the Owner

The project owner sets the vision and creates a new project on Kolabriq with clear goals and expectations.

2. Appoint a Project Manager

To ensure smooth coordination, the owner assigns a project manager to lead and manage the workflow.

3. Build the Team & Distribute Tasks

The project manager invites team members and distributes tasks — assisted by AI for smart and fair delegation.

4. Get Work Done with AI Support

Each team member completes their tasks. AI assists with scheduling, prioritization, and reminders to stay on track.

5. Earn Rewards for Your Contribution

Once all assigned tasks are completed, each member earns their individual reward — your effort never goes unnoticed.
